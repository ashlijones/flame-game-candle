Welcome to Flame Game Candles! I use high quality 100% soy wax. I hand pour each and everyone of the candles and wax melts that I sell. I customize each candle and wax melt to what you want! I let you choose the tin, a scent and a wax color if you would like. I also offer some glitter to add to spice it up just a bit and put more of a wow factor to your customized wax. I want you to be able to get exactly what you want. I never mass produce candles because in my shop, the choice is yours.

Why 100% soy wax? Is there a difference between the wax? To that I say yes... definitely yes!!!

  • Soy wax is biodegradable and cleans up with plain old soap and water.
  • Soy wax has a lower melting point than paraffin wax(the store bought candles have this kind of wax) and because of this, soy candles will burn slower or longer than paraffin candles.
  • Soy wax burns with zero petro soot, creating petro soot free candles. Paraffin candles burn with petro-soot.
  • The Soy Wax is a natural moisturizer and is perfect for sealing in moisture a in your skin. So if you spill some onto your skin just rub it in and you will be set.
  • Soy Wax is vegan.
